About our hotel

Best place for enjoying pure vegeterian food

Our hotel has a history of 60 yrs old & the popular item on the menu .The enterprise was started by kota Balakrishna prabhu and now it is continued by his son venkatesh prabhu and grand son Rajesh prabhu .Our hotel is vegeterian rastaurant with quality food at reasonable price includes north and south indian food. Many politician and famous personalities have visited our hotel. Our hotel is famous for idle vada , semige with coconut rasa, akki rotti , moode, kadabu ,crispy uddina ambade and special menthe dosa served with butter.Meal includes ragi balls served with bassaru and pathrode at 60 Rs . Evening snacks like podi, sukkin unde, madhur vade and chats and mundakki upkari , manchries and chana puri which is famous for. In Juice we have Navarathna and Rasayana served with cashew are famous and we also have special gudbad .
